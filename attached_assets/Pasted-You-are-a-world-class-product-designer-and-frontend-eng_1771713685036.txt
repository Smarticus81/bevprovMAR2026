You are a world-class product designer and frontend engineer. 
Design a modern, minimalist, premium landing page for a platform called “BevOne”.

BevOne is a unified AI platform that allows event venues to create their own voice-powered AI web apps. It includes three flagship assistants:
- BevPro POS (voice-powered point of sale)
- BevPro Inventory
- Venue Assistant (booking, admin, finance, oversight)

The landing page must feel calm, intelligent, and inevitable. No loud gradients. No neon cyberpunk. No generic SaaS illustrations.

Aesthetic direction:
- Ultra-minimalist
- Soft neutral palette (off-white, champagne beige, matte charcoal, subtle stone grays)
- High-end typography (clean geometric sans-serif)
- Generous white space
- Glassmorphism used sparingly and elegantly
- Subtle motion, not distracting

Hero Section:
- Fullscreen background animation: extremely close-up macro view of champagne bubbles rising along the inside of a glass.
- The bubbles should feel abstract and cinematic — soft focus, warm highlights, shallow depth of field.
- Animation should loop smoothly and feel luxurious.
- Slight blur overlay so text remains readable.
- No loud sparkle effects.
